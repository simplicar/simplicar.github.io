Simplicar

Simplicar is an automotive website for new car buyers. It is an exclusive car buying platform for affinity group members. Through a network of certified dealers it aims to find you the best price and benefits transparent information, but mostly, a great car buying expercience.

Positions

We are looking for semi-senior ninjas with ~ 2-3 years of experience who want to experience the adrenaline of working on a startup, the true rollercoster, which is rapidly growing and expanding the business across the region.

The positions are for our offices in Buenos Aires in Retiro (Plaza San Martin), and Vicente Lopez, but you can also work remotely if you need it. We offer a great salary according to your experience and horsepower, a great health insurance plan, and of course, great discounts in your next new car :)

We believe this is an excellent opportunity as we are starting to build the development team and the culture of the company and there's still plenty of opportunities to make huge impact on the business.

Hope to hear from you.

Front-end developer

We heavily use front-end technologies like AngularJS to develop modern web applications for our users. We are currently doing some research on ReactJS and ES6 to implement a new feature which will make a huge impact on the business.

As a front-end developer you will be responisble for the development of UI components across all of our projects.

Must-have
  • Expert knowledge of JavaScript & AngularJS (or at least one other MVC library / framework like BackboneJS, EmberJS, etc.)
  • Expert knowledge of object-oriented JavaScript (pure JavaScript, DOM API) and design patterns
  • Expert knowledge of HTML, cross-browser CSS
  • Ability to write high-performance, reusable code for UI components
  • Understanding of RESTful application architecture
Nice to have
  • Experience using git
  • Experience with TDD/automated testing, CI and any unit testing framework (Jasmine, Mocha, etc.)
  • Experience with working with Agile \ SCRUM

Full-stack developer

Most of our projects use Ruby as the main programming language. As we grow in users and features we need to develop well-tested, clean and performance focused code.

As a full-stack developer you will be responsible of developing mostly server-side code across all of our projects. You will be in charge of optimizing the applications at a database, application and network level and deploying and configuring applications.

Must-have
  • At least 2 years experience working with Ruby or other object oriented programming language
  • Excellent knowledge of object-oriented programming and design
  • Understanding of RESTful application architecture
  • Good relational database experience (MySQL, Postgres)
Nice to have
  • Experience using git
  • Knowledge of JavaScript & Angular JS (or at least one other MVC library / framework like Backbone, EmberJS etc.)
  • Experience with TDD/automated testing, CI and any unit testing framework (RSpec, Minitest, TesUnit, etc.)
  • Experience deploying applications. We use Heroku and capistrano to deploy our applications
  • Experience with working with Agile/SCRUM
